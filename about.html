Who Is Nahtanriel?

Nahtanriel is a seasoned guitarist, singer-songwriter, and independent musician based in Sunbury, Victoria—a vibrant town on the outskirts of Melbourne, Australia. Known for his rich vocal tones and emotive guitar work, Nahtanriel brings heartfelt authenticity to every performance, whether on stage, in the studio, or on the streets as a dedicated busker.

Nahtanriel's journey began at the age of 16, performing in his high school class band, where his passion for music quickly took root. He later refined his craft at Melbourne Polytechnic, earning a Bachelor of Music Performance. Following his graduation, he independently produced and released two original albums, demonstrating his versatility as a songwriter and producer.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Nahtanriel became a source of resilience and positivity for his community. Wearing a protective mask and cordoning off a safe space in the Sunbury town centre, he continued busking and offering live music to uplift locals during one of the most challenging periods in recent history. His dedication drew widespread recognition, leading to regular bookings for private events and performances at pubs, cafés, festivals, and charity events across Melbourne.

Nahtanriel’s music has been featured on Sunbury Radio, where he performed two of his original tracks, further cementing his presence in the local scene. He is currently in the process of producing his third album—continuing to evolve his sound while staying true to his roots. Whether delivering dynamic, high-energy sets for large audiences or intimate acoustic moments tailored for private gatherings, Nahtanriel creates performances that leave a lasting impression.
